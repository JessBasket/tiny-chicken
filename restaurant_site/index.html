About Midna's

At Midna's Restaurant, our mission is simple: provide great food and service. We start with locally sourced ingredients and use them to create delicious meals to be enjoyed by all. Come and enjoy our spacious dining room which seats up to 75 guests and includes a bar serving local brews. Our friendly and courteous wait staff is always ready to serve you!
